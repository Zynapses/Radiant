### Execution Modes

| Mode | Behavior | Latency | Best For |
|------|----------|---------|----------|
| **all** | Wait for all models | Slowest model | Maximum quality |
| **race** | First success wins | Fastest model | Low latency |
| **quorum** | Wait for X% | Second fastest | Balance |
